ABOUT US

CashTrack helps you in keeping track of your expenditure. It shows you the amount you have in your balance of three types (Cash, Debit and Credit). You can enter the details of all your transactions. All the transactions will be shown in the history page. You can later update or delete transaction data. Various graphs showing the your expenditure will be displayed in the dashboard.

Uses of CashTrack:

  • It is devoid of human errors.
  • It provides analytics of expenditure.
  • Maintenance of physical record of expenditure is not required.
  • It is more secure.
  • It deprives the user of the difficulty involved in keeping the data in his/her memory.
  • History of transactions can be viewed at any time.
  • Motivates the user to have control over his/her spending habits.
  • There would be no need to hire an account manager.
  • This application is user-friendly.
